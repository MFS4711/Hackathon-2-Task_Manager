About

FocusFlow is a powerful task management app designed to help you stay on top of your personal and professional goals. With features like prioritisation, categorisation, and status tracking, you can organise tasks and meet deadlines without stress.

Whether you're managing work projects, personal errands, or study goals, FocusFlow adapts to your needs and helps you stay focused and productive.

Create Tasks

Create tasks with clear titles, descriptions, and deadlines. Organise them into categories like Work, Personal, or Study.

Prioritise

Set the priority of each task to ensure you focus on what matters most. Choose from high, medium, or low priority.

Track Progress

Mark your tasks as "In Progress" and "Completed". Tasks that are overdue are automatically flagged for you to address.
